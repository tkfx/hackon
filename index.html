What is Hackon?

HackOn! is a gathering of professionals and students that encourages entrepreneurship and innovation in Israelâ€™s north. HackOn! is a great opportunity to make new connections, form a team, build a web or mobile application, receive professional guidance from our mentors and work on cool creative projects. HackOn! is the first ever Hackathon held at the Technion, a shared initiative of Google Developers Group Haifa and Nazareth.

Who can apply?

Students, professionals, developers, designers, IE majors, CS majors, EE majors, math and more! As long as you have something you are passionate about, an itch to build something amazing and collaborate in a team of talented individuals. We can only accommodate 70 participants, make it count!
